There are various bus routes you can take to reach La Trobe.

Tram

Tram 86 from the city stops at the Plenty Road entrance to the Melbourne (Bundoora) Campus. The tram stop is only a few minutes walk from the centre of the University.

Train

You can also catch a train to come to the Bundoora Campus but you need to transfer to a bus. Check the following information to find your best fit!

  • Mernda Line
  • Hurstbridge Line

For more detailed information, visit La Trobe public transport

To plan your travel, visit PTV Journey Planner

Parking

Parking should not be a problem at La Trobe Univeristy if you commute by car. Parking is all available around the campus. Access the map below to locate the parking lot that is most convenient for you on the campus by clicking on the image.
